Workplace Safety Documentation

Understanding workplace safety documentation is crucial for forklift operators. This module covers the essential documents and resources that provide safety information in the workplace.

Key Safety Documentation Sources

  • WHS Policies and Procedures: These documents outline the organization's commitment to workplace health and safety and provide specific guidelines for safe work practices.
  • Forklift Operator Manual: The manufacturer's guide containing specific information about the forklift's operation, maintenance, and safety features.
  • Safe Working Procedures: Step-by-step instructions for performing specific tasks safely, including pre-operational checks and emergency procedures.

Important Note

Always ensure you have access to and understand these documents before operating a forklift. Regular review of safety documentation helps maintain a safe working environment.

Practice Questions

What are three (3) documented sources you could refer to for workplace safety information?

The three main documented sources for workplace safety information are: WHS policies and procedures, Forklift operator manual, and Safe working procedures.
