TLILIC0003 - Licence to Operate a Forklift Truck

1. What are three (3) documented sources you could refer to for workplace safety information?

1. WHS Act and Regulations
2. Australian Standards
3. Codes of Practice

2. What are three (3) duties an employer should take to ensure the health and safety of a worker?

1. Provide a safe work environment
2. Provide training and supervision
3. Ensure safe equipment and procedures

3. After successfully obtaining your High Risk Work Licence, list two (2) things your employer must provide prior to you operating an unfamiliar forklift.

1. Training on the specific forklift
2. Information about workplace hazards

4. What does the Duty of Care as a worker involve?

Following safety rules, using PPE, reporting hazards

5. What are two (2) things the work health and safety regulator could do if it is found that you were not working safely as a High Risk Work Licence holder?

1. Issue a warning or suspend the licence
2. Impose a fine or revoke the licence

6. List three (3) people you should talk to about workplace hazards before starting work.

1. Supervisor
2. Colleagues in the same area
3. Safety officer

7. Why is it important to consult with others about workplace hazards?

To identify and eliminate risks, ensuring workplace safety
